Mission

To strive relentlessly to build inter-community solidarity nets, leading to peaceful coexistence in Lebanon.

Partnering with Nawraj

  • Partnerships and collaborations are built with like-mind civil society organizations sharing the same goals and dedicated to bringing significant change through sustainable development initiatives.
  • Partnerships are developed based on trust, credibility, efficiency and complementarity to optimize our impact.
  • Collaborations with private sector are effective and based on the Corporate Citizenship approach, acknowledging the role of private enterprises in community development.

There are many ways to help us make a difference in the lives of communities severely impacted by the multiple crises prevailing in Lebanon.

SPONSORSHIP
Sponsoring a project
Join our circle of friends and support new and on-going projects across the country.

Nawraj implements two models for project engagement, which include:
1. Establishing privately owned community-based companies or entities for specific projects.
2. Consolidating existing local private and community projects.

These models are based on the following principles:
  • Involving locals in identifying and assessing the region's needs.
  • Ensuring the active participation of women in achieving the advancement of society.
  • Creating and sustaining local job opportunities

PARTNERSHIP
Partnering with Nawraj

  • Partnerships and collaborations are built with like-minded civil society organizations sharing the same goals and dedicated to bringing significant change through sustainable development initiatives.
  • Partnerships are developed based on trust, credibility, efficiency and complementarity to optimize our impact.
  • Collaborations mean improvement and better chances of success through the exchange of expertise, ideas and resources.

INTERNSHIP
Build experience with Nawraj

Nawraj provides internships to students from diverse backgrounds who are interested in social and economic development projects. These internships offer students with practical experience, enabling them to explore their potential and achieve personal growth. The knowledge and experience gained from these internships can help students consolidate their academic program and advance their professional careers.

VOLUNTEERING
Join our team of volunteers

We welcome new people with different skills and backgrounds to support vulnerable and underserved communities.
Volunteering contributes to the greater good. At the same time, it represents a unique opportunity for personal growth and development, providing a sense of purpose, fulfillment, and belonging.
If you're interested in making a positive impact, we have a variety of opportunities available in our social program.

Donating to Nawraj

By donating to Nawraj, you have the opportunity to make a tangible impact on crucial projects that focus on education, healthcare, and employment creation. Your contribution will go directly towards the consolidation and implementation of these initiatives, ensuring their success and positively impacting the lives of many.

Our track record speaks for itself:

  • Over 100 projects successfully implemented.
  • Our work covers the entire Lebanese territory particularly underserved communities. in long-neglected remote areas.
  • Our initiatives are developed, conducted and supervised by our team of experts.
